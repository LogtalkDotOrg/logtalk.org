Community Support

Community supported open discussion forums and chat room are available.

Commercial Support

For Logtalk commercial support options visit the logtalk.pt website.

Logtalk News Mailing List

You may subscribe the Logtalk News (announcements-only) mailing list using this form. This is a low volume mailing list, used for Logtalk announcements such as new releases, bug fixes, and Logtalk-related events.

Bug Reports

Please visit the Logtalk developers website and open a ticket describing the problem found. You may also send your bug report by mail.

Registration

You may register as a Logtalk user by using this form. Please register if you use Logtalk!

Suggestions

Please visit the Logtalk developers website and open a ticket with your suggestion. You may also send your suggestions for the next Logtalk release using this form or by mail.
